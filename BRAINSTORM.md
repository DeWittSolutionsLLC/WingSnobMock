# Wing Snob Website Eye-Catching Ideas Brainstorm

## Analysis of Current Design
- **Generic patterns found:** Standard card grids with borders/shadows, typical section ordering, muted gradients
- **Heavy card usage:** Menu items, Weekly Deals, Why Wing Snob, Franchise metrics all use boxed layouts
- **Good existing elements:** Sticky scrollytelling, sauce marquee, staggered animations, parallax images

---

## 🎯 HIGH-IMPACT IDEAS

### 1. Bold Typography & Overlaps
**Goal:** Break the rigid grid and create magazine-style drama

- Giant overlapping headlines that float over images (like "WINGS" behind a wing photo)
- Text-to-image reveals where text "uncovers" photos as you scroll
- Kinetic typography that scales dramatically on scroll
- Split-screen text: half on image, half in background

**Where to apply:** Hero, Weekly Deals, Menu headers, Sauce Lab

---

### 2. Visual Texture & Atmosphere
**Goal:** Add restaurant/food atmosphere, not generic web feel

- **Smoke/steam effects** rising from food photos (CSS particle effects)
- **Sauce splatter animations** on hover - like sauce was flung onto the card
- **Grain/noise texture overlay** on dark sections for depth
- **Char/Grill marks** CSS patterns on edges
- **Hot oil shimmer** animation on background gradients

**Where to apply:** Sauce cards, Menu photos, Hero background, CTA sections

---

### 3. Interactive Sauce Experience
**Goal:** Make the 20 sauces the highlight of the site

- **Sauce Selector Wheel** - circular drag interface to browse by heat
- **Color-gradient background** that changes as you filter (mild = yellow, hot = red)
- **Sauce "splash" reveal** - clicking a sauce triggers a splash animation
- **Heat wave animation** radiating from hot sauces
- **Real-time sauce mixer** - select 2 sauces and see the blend color

**Where to apply:** Menu Sauce Lab section, Homepage Sauce Preview

---

### 4. Layout Innovation
**Goal:** Break the cookie-cutter section pattern

- **Diagonal section joins** - slash through sections at angles instead of horizontal lines
- **Horizontal scroll section** for "How It Works" - walk through horizontally like a story
- **Bento grid boxes** - varying sized boxes like Pinterest/masonry (not all equal cards)
- **Overlapping cards without borders** - layered depth, not boxed
- **Parallax layered sections** - foreground food, mid text, background texture

**Where to apply:** Weekly Deals grid, Franchise metrics, Perks tiers

---

### 5. Motion & Animation Rich
**Goal:** Make everything feel alive and premium

- **Floating food** - continuous gentle float animation (already has photo-float, expand it)
- **Marquee everywhere** - continuous scrolling for deals, sides, reviews
- **Scroll-triggered reveals** - elements fly in from unexpected directions
- **Stagger cascade** - cards cascade in one-by-one, not all at once
- **Text scramble** - hover over titles to see letters "scramble" to new text

**Where to apply:** All sections,

---

### 6. Unique Photo Treatment
**Goal:** Make photos feel like食欲 (appetite-inducing)

- **3D card tilt** - hover cards to see slight 3D perspective shift

---

### 7. Branded Micro-interactions
**Goal:** Custom interactions that feel "Wing Snob"

- **"Sauce Boss" meter** - scrolling shows progress toward free wings
- **Heat Level Toggle** - switch to see all mild/hot options instantly
- **Wing counter** - animated wing pieces counting up (like 70+ locations)
- **Combo builder preview** - drag items to build your order visually
- **Stamp card loyalty** - digital stamp card that fills as you scroll

**Where to apply:** Homepage stats, Perks section, Order flow

---

### 8. Color & Theme Shifts
**Goal:** Make each section feel distinct, not same gradient

- **Dark/Light mode toggles** per section (not whole page)
- **Heat-gradient backgrounds** - cool blue → warm yellow → fiery red based on content
- **Gold accent moments** - strategic gold highlights for CTAs
- **Night vs Day sections** - some sections dark restaurant feel, others light airy

**Where to apply:** Sections with different moods (Catering = gold, Perks = dark vibe)
